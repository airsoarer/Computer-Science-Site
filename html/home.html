About

Riley High School is proud to host South Bend's only Computer Science Pathway program. We offer Introduction to Computer Science, Computer Science 1, Computer Science 2, Computer Science 2: Android App Development, Computer Science 3: Cybersecurity, and Computer Science 3: Software Development. There are other courses in the works as well.

On this page you will find the best of Riley's Computer Science projects, ranging from Scratch projects and websites made by CS1 students, to full-blown Android Apps developed by CS3: Software Development students. Feel free to look around and try out these student-created projects. Any questions or comments can be directed to Kristen Haubold

About the Teacher


Kristen Haubold

Kristen Haubold has been teaching Computer Science at Riley High School for 5 years. She is currently teaching Computer Science 1 (CS Principles), Computer Science 2 (CS A), Cybersecurity, and an independent study CS class. Ms. Haubold's background is in Mathematics Education and Computer Education. She is a 2013 graduate of Indiana University Bloomington and loves to call South Bend home. Ms. Haubold also works part time at South Bend Code School as their Director of Curriculum and Instruction.
